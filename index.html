Welcome to Ozamiz

Ozamiz City, located in Misamis Occidental, Philippines, is a charming coastal city known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and scenic beauty. Nestled between Panguil Bay and Mount Malindang, it offers stunning natural landscapes, including lush mountains and serene waters. Key attractions include the historical Fort Santiago, the vibrant Cotta Shrine, and the picturesque Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel Adventure Park. Ozamiz is also a hub for trade and commerce in the region, blending modern progress with its traditional charm.

Mission

To promote sustainable development and improve the quality of life for the people of Ozamiz City through effective governance, inclusive growth, and the preservation of its cultural and natural heritage.

Vission

Ozamiz City aims to be a progressive, environmentally sustainable, and culturally rich city, fostering a thriving community with opportunities for all.

Core Values

We uphold integrity by being honest and transparent in all actions, promote sustainability through eco-friendly practices, foster a strong community built on inclusivity and harmony, strive for excellence in all endeavors, and dedicate ourselves to service by putting the needs of the people first with compassion and commitment.
