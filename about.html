About Ozamiz

Ozamiz City, located in the northern part of Mindanao, Philippines, is a vibrant and dynamic city known for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning natural landscapes. It serves as the capital of Misamis Occidental and is strategically positioned between the beautiful Panguil Bay and the towering Mount Malindang. The city offers a mix of modern development and traditional charm, with key attractions like the historic Fort Santiago, Cotta Shrine, and the scenic Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel Adventure Park. Ozamiz is also a hub for trade, commerce, and agriculture, providing opportunities for growth and progress while maintaining a deep connection to its cultural roots. The city's welcoming community and growing infrastructure make it an ideal place to visit, live, and invest.

Ozamiz Famous For

Ozamiz City is famous for its rich history, scenic landscapes, and vibrant culture. Notable attractions include Fort Santiago, a historical site from the Spanish era, the Cotta Shrine commemorating the city's heritage, Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel Adventure Park with stunning views, Panguil Bay, a key harbor for trade, and its thriving agricultural industry, especially in coconut and sugar production.
